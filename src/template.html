About me

I've been writing software in some form or another for almost 35 years, and it shows.

I got my start with a RadioShack TRS-80, given to me as a birthday present, along with two thick books on BASIC. My grandfather, who had been involved with old punch card mainframes, told me, "Computers are the future. Learn how to use this." Now, if you're familiar with the TRS-80, you'll already know this, but it didn't come installed with any sort of software other than the BASIC language. Thus, if I wanted to do anything with it, I needed to learn how to write software.

I threw myself into it with a passion and discovered that I had a true natural talent for understanding code. Have you ever had a friend who was a particularly talented musician? The kind who can pick up an instrument and, with a month of seemingly effortless practice, be better than a person who has been playing for years? That's how I am with code. Programming languages simply make sense to me. I learn new ones incredibly quickly, and then apply all the previous lessons I've learned to truly excel with them.

On a personal note, I'm the proud father of two amazing girls who are way too smart for their own good. Together, we love playing all kinds of games (video, board, card). I'm also a passionate consumer of pop culture. I love movies and music and can talk about both for hours on end.
